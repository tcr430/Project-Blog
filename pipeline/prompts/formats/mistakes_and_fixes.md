Format: Mistakes and Fixes

Write this article as a mistakes-and-fixes guide for one decor trend only.

Section intent:
- Introduction: explain why this trend can go wrong without planning.
- Sections 1-5: each section should cover one common mistake and a practical fix.
- For each fix, explain why it works visually and how to apply it with real materials/colors/textures.
- Conclusion: recap the biggest pitfalls to avoid and a simple action plan.

Keep advice constructive and specific, not alarmist.
