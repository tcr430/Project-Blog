Format: Ideas Article

Write this article as an ideas-led piece for one decor trend only.

Section intent:
- Introduction: set the mood and define the trend briefly.
- Sections 1-5: each section should present one distinct, actionable decorating idea.
- Each idea should include visual reasoning, materials/colors/textures, and practical implementation details.
- Conclusion: help readers choose the first idea to try based on their space and budget.

Avoid overlap between ideas and keep each section distinct.
