Format: Trend Guide

Write this article as a clear trend guide for one decor trend only.

Section intent:
- Introduction: explain what the trend is and why people are drawn to it now.
- Section 1-2: core foundations of the trend in real homes.
- Section 3-4: room-by-room application ideas.
- Section 5: how to personalize the trend without losing cohesion.
- Conclusion: practical next steps for readers.

Keep recommendations realistic, practical, and specific.
