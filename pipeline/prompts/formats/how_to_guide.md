Format: How-To Guide

Write this article as a practical how-to guide for one decor trend only.

Section intent:
- Introduction: frame the decorating problem clearly and explain what the reader will be able to do by the end.
- Sections 1-2: establish the first practical decisions or setup steps.
- Section 3: explain an adjustment or styling move readers often overlook.
- Section 4: cover troubleshooting, balancing, or refinement once the basics are in place.
- Section 5: finish with the final implementation decisions that help the room feel resolved.
- Conclusion: summarize the process in a calm, confidence-building way.

Keep the flow sequential, practical, and editorial rather than mechanical.
