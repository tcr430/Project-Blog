Image Prompt QA Checklist

For each generated prompt, verify:
- It clearly describes an interior scene aligned with the section context.
- It explicitly implies editorial interior photography.
- It explicitly or clearly implies natural daylight.
- It mentions realistic materials and textures.
- It includes restrictions for no text, no logos, and no people.
- It avoids contradictory or vague visual direction.

Output format (for future automation):
- pass: boolean
- issues: list of short strings
- notes: short summary
