<div class="content-column privacy-page">
  <p class="page-intro">This Privacy Policy explains how information may be handled when you visit and interact with this website.</p>

  <h2>Introduction</h2>
  <p>The Livin' Edit is an editorial-style website that publishes decor content and related media. As a general rule, the site does not intentionally collect personal data directly from visitors unless information is explicitly provided, such as through a future contact form, newsletter signup, or similar direct interaction.</p>

  <h2>Information We Collect</h2>
  <p>The site itself is designed to minimize direct data collection. In normal browsing, we do not intentionally request or store personal information unless you choose to provide it. Depending on how the site evolves, limited information such as an email address or message content may be submitted voluntarily through third-party tools or forms.</p>

  <h2>Third-Party Services</h2>
  <p>Third-party services may collect technical, device, or usage-related information when you visit the site or click related links. These services may include GitHub Pages, analytics providers, embedded media providers, Buy Me a Coffee, newsletter or form services, affiliate networks, or other linked tools and platforms that may be used over time.</p>
  <p>Because those services operate independently, their data practices are governed by their own privacy policies rather than this site alone.</p>

  <h2>Cookies and Tracking Technologies</h2>
  <p>The site itself does not intentionally rely on a complex in-house tracking system. However, third-party services used for hosting, analytics, affiliate attribution, embeds, or external links may use cookies, pixels, or similar tracking technologies to support functionality, measure traffic, or attribute referrals.</p>

  <h2>External Links</h2>
  <p>This site may contain links to external websites, products, platforms, and services. Once you leave this site, any information you share or any data collected is subject to the privacy practices of the external service you are using. Visitors should review the privacy policies of those third parties directly.</p>

  <h2>Data Retention</h2>
  <p>Because the site does not intentionally collect significant personal data directly in its current form, it does not maintain a broad independent retention program for visitor information. If direct submissions are introduced later through third-party providers, any retention of that data will typically depend on the relevant provider and the operational need for the communication.</p>

  <h2>Your Rights</h2>
  <p>Depending on your location, you may have rights relating to personal information processed by third-party services connected to the site, including rights to access, correction, deletion, or objection. If you have questions about data handled through a third-party tool, the most effective step is usually to review that provider's privacy documentation and contact them directly where appropriate.</p>

  <h2>Contact</h2>
  <p>If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or about how the site operates, you can contact us at <a href="mailto:{{ site.newsletter_email }}">{{ site.newsletter_email }}</a>.</p>
